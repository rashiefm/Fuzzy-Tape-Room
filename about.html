Who We Are ?

Fuzzy Tape Room is a 'studio' engaged in Audio and Information Technology which was established in 2021. Fuzzy Tape Room itself uses the word Fuzzy which in the audio itself is taken from the word Fuzz which means "distorted effect and 'blur' which is produced electrically in sound through increased vibration or added tone ''. In terms of IT itself is taken from the Fuzzy Logic terminology used in Artificial Intelligence, and Tape Room itself inspired studio from East Nashville, Tennessee called Tiny Tape Room. These there correlates are used for naming company / studio. Fuzzy Tape Room has products and services in the field of audio and information technology such as Sound Design and Web Design.

Sound Design

Process of recording, developing, or producing audio elements from Pre-Production to Post-Production. In the process of sound design there are sound effect and foley effect for support what sound design need.

Sound Enhancement

The process is almost entirely composed of filtering away unwanted sounds in order to leave a more understandable version.

Editing

Editing services include Mixing and Mastering. Mixing is the process of optimizing and combining multitrack recordings into a final mono, stereo or surround the sound product while mastering is a final mix to a data storage device (the master)

So that's us. There's no other way to put it.
